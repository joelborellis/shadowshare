Solution Overview

ComboCurve is a next-generation, cloud-based forecasting and economics platform designed for the Oil & Gas industry. By integrating type curve creation, probabilistic forecasting, reporting, and data management, ComboCurve significantly reduces the time it takes to analyze wells and portfolios—from weeks to minutes. Its advanced automation, user-friendly interface, and robust analytics toolkit enable accurate and efficient decision-making.

The high-level value proposition lies in minimizing manual data handling, breaking through silos across departments, and democratizing advanced forecasting capabilities—so engineers, business analysts, and executives can collaborate in real time. ComboCurve empowers users to quickly assess multiple scenarios, keep forecasts up-to-date with real-time data integrations, and drive better strategic decisions.

Target Market

While ComboCurve’s technology can benefit a wide range of users within the energy sector, the primary target market includes:

  • Independent Oil & Gas Operators looking for rapid well forecasting and streamlined portfolio evaluations.
  • Large E&P companies needing advanced enterprise-level reserves reporting and evaluation tools.
  • Private Equity groups making critical investment decisions by modeling acquisitions accurately.
  • Reservoir Engineers, Petroleum Engineers, and Data Technicians who require integrated forecasting and economic analyses.
  • Bankers, Advisors, and Midstream professionals aiming to evaluate deals faster with accurate data-driven insights.

Target Personas

Key roles and typical pain points:

  • Reservoir Engineers & Petroleum Engineers: Overburdened by manual forecasting in outdated tools, leading to process inefficiencies. They need a streamlined, user-friendly platform to handle large datasets quickly.
  • Data Technicians / Engineering Technicians: Struggling with time-consuming data uploads and conversions between legacy software. They need robust integrations to centralize large data sets and reduce repetitive manual tasks.
  • Executives (CEOs, CFOs, VPs): Require fast, accurate insights and scenario planning to make strategic financial decisions. They want clarity and agility when accessing cross-department data.
  • Finance & Private Equity Stakeholders: Concerned about inaccurate data leading to risky investments. They want credible, data-backed forecasts and valuations to underpin confident decision-making.

Functional titles that care about these pain points: Reservoir Engineer, Production Engineer, Petroleum Engineer, Engineering Technician, CFO, VP of Finance, VP of Operations, Corporate Development Associate, A&D specialist.

Value ComboCurve brings: Reliability and speed, automated data processes, advanced forecasting accuracy, reduced risk in budget planning, and streamlined cross-team collaboration.

Suggested questions to ask these personas:

  • How do you currently manage and update large well datasets, especially across multiple software platforms?
  • What is your biggest challenge in evaluating new deals or tracking existing well performance?
  • Are you satisfied with the speed and accuracy of your current forecasting method?
  • How do internal stakeholders (finance, executive leadership) access and interpret your team’s forecasts?

Competitive Differentiators

  • Automation & Speed: ComboCurve can auto-forecast large well sets in minutes, significantly faster than traditional reservoir engineering tools.
  • Scalability & Cloud-Based: No hardware constraints or lengthy installation; offers real-time collaboration on automatically synced data in the cloud.
  • Versatility: Built-in support for drilling schedules, advanced type curve generation, scenario planning, and net-zero emissions forecasting.
  • Integration & Data Management: ComboSync seamlessly connects external data sources (Enverus, IHS) and merges them into one platform, eliminating cumbersome manual uploads.
  • Ease of Use & Customer Support: An intuitive interface matched with responsive Customer Success Engineers ensures quick adoption and minimal learning curve.

First Call Structure

Objective: Create immediate interest and preference for ComboCurve by leveraging industry expertise and real proof points, then qualify the prospect’s needs for follow-on action.

Suggested Structure

  1. Introduction & Credibility:
    • Highlight ComboCurve’s role as a modern, efficient O&G forecasting and economics platform used successfully by top operators.
    • Use reference examples such as EQT’s success, focusing on speed and ROI benefits.
  2. Discovery:
    • Ask about how they currently manage data and forecasting. "What are your biggest bottlenecks in forecasting or evaluating deals?"
    • Gauge readiness for change. "Have you considered a cloud-based solution to unify your forecasting and economics?"
  3. Proof & Alignment:
    • Sum up how ComboCurve addresses their biggest challenge: "We help you reduce manual processes, drastically cut forecasting time, and unify your data into one easy-to-manage platform."
    • Demonstrate with real data or a short example how quickly forecasts can be generated.
  4. Qualification:
    • Assess timelines, budget constraints, and potential internal champions or blockers: "Who else on your team would need to be involved in exploring a new forecasting solution?"
    • Confirm seriousness: "Would you be willing to walk me through your current well evaluation workflow and share an example dataset to see how ComboCurve can integrate seamlessly?"
  5. Next Steps:
    • Propose the next meeting or stakeholder introduction. "How about setting up a time next week to do a brief demonstration with your engineering team?"
    • Agree on an action item for both sides. "I can prepare a custom demonstration using one of your well datasets if you can provide me with basic production data."

Key Listening Points

  • Are they struggling with lengthy forecasting times or archaic software integration?
  • Is there immediate pressure to reduce overhead, unify data, or push for better collaboration between teams?
  • Do they have upcoming deadlines for reserve reporting or an acquisition that requires fast, accurate forecasts?

Scripted Statement to Demonstrate Expertise

“In speaking with operators across the country, we see that using multiple disconnected tools often slows viability checks for new deals, leaving teams with outdated information. At ComboCurve, we automate well forecasting, tie economics directly to real-time data, and give you the power to run multiple scenarios in minutes. This positions your team to be significantly more agile.”

Ideal Client Profile and Market Intelligence

The Ideal Client Profile (ICP) for ComboCurve typically includes exploration and production companies, non-op and minerals buyers, and midstream operators seeking to optimize processes across large well datasets. Prospective clients handle robust data flows from multiple external sources and face the need to unify and accelerate forecasting and investment decisions.

Key attributes of the ICP:

  • Operates or evaluates a large number of wells (hundreds to tens of thousands).
  • Understands the need to streamline forecasting and reduce manual data entry.
  • Has multiple data sources and is looking for a single platform to unify these inputs.
  • Prioritizes speed and collaboration to evaluate and close deals swiftly.
  • Seeks advanced analytics for emissions tracking or net-zero planning (often relevant for larger operators).

Market Intelligence: The Oil & Gas tech space is pivoting toward digital transformation, focusing on cloud-based platforms for real-time data integration, GHG emissions forecasting, and robust scenario planning. ComboCurve is positioned to capitalize on operators’ need for an all-in-one system that unifies previously siloed workflows.
